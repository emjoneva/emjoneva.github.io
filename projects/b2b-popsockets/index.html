CASE: POPSOCKETS B2B SITE REFRESH

How to best introduce the concept of promotional PopSockets to B2B customers?

My role: UX designer
Team members: eCom Coordinator, EMEA B2B Director and PopSockets Shopify Developers

Problem statements at the start of the project

The following problem statements were identified by the owners of the project (EMEA eCom Coordinator and EMEA B2B Director)

1. Benefits of promotional PopSockets products were unclear to B2B customers, especially SMBs

2. The ordering process was confusing for users and pricing of items was not transparent from the start

Top skills used during the project

  • communication and collaboration in diverse teams
  • combining business goals and user needs
  • wireframing and prototyping

Tools used during the project
Communication: Slack, Jira, Asana
Tools: Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Shopify
Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
